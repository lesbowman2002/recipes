Ingredients:
 - 3 packages of cornbread
 - 1 1/2 - 2 cup chopped yellow onion
 - 1 cup chopped celery
 - 2 tablespoons sage
 - 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
 - 5 slices of white bread
 - 3 eggs
 - 2 cups milk
 - 3 - 4 cups chicken broth
 -  2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
 1. Make packages of corn bread as directions on package.
 2. Break of cornbreak into large mixing bowl in tiny pieces. Add pieces of white bread cut up with cornbread.
 3. Saute onions and celery on low heat with 2 tbl of butter
 4. Add sage, black pepper, milk, and broth until a moist consistency.
 5. Pour into baking pan and bake about 2-3 hours or until edges brown and firm.
